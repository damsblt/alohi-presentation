PR & Thought Leadership

Strategy
  • Leverage PR to build credibility and reach wider audiences without heavy ad spend.
  • Announce milestones and innovations via press releases and media outreach.
  • Pitch successes to tech press and local business journals.
Planned PR Activities
  • Quarterly press releases:
    • Q1: Announce new integration/partnership
    • Q2: Highlight customer success story/case study
    • Q3: PR campaign for major product update/innovation
    • Q4: CEO thought leadership (op-ed/interview)
  • Submit for awards and seek speaking opportunities at conferences.
Budget
  • CHF 10,000 (press release distribution, PR consultant, travel for speaking events)
KPIs:
  • 5+ media mentions/press articles per year
  • 20% spike in direct traffic after press releases
  • At least one external thought leadership piece published
  • Monitor share of voice vs. competitors
