Objectives & Strategy Overview

Unify Brand & Growth Goals
  • Promote Sign.Plus, Fax.Plus, and Scan.Plus as one integrated ecosystem (post-2024 rebranding)
  • Target +25% user growth (from 4M to ~5M users)
  • Increase adoption in enterprise segments
Strategic Focus
  • Highlight strengths: security, compliance, seamless integrations, productivity
  • Emphasize Swiss data privacy and certifications (ISO 27001, HIPAA, SOC 2)
  • Use "Work that flows" philosophy to position products as intuitive and efficient
Integrated Ecosystem Approach
  • One account/login for all products
  • Encourage cross-usage (e.g., attract users with free Scan.Plus, upsell Fax.Plus and Sign.Plus)
  • Reinforce "scan, sign or fax in one place" in all communications
KPIs (12-month goals):
  • +50% website traffic
  • +20% conversion of free users to paid plans
  • 100+ new enterprise leads (15% conversion to customers)
  • Improved brand awareness (track via media mentions and direct traffic growth)
