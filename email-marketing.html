Email Marketing & Automation

Strategy
  • Implement email nurturing and marketing automation to improve conversion and cross-sell.
  • Segment email lists by product and user type for tailored content.
  • Create onboarding and drip campaigns for new users and trial sign-ups.
Tactics
  • Send a monthly newsletter with product updates, security tips, and blog highlights.
  • Set up automated emails for upsell, re-engagement, and lead nurturing.
  • Utilize marketing automation tools to track interactions and score leads.
Budget
  • CHF 5,000 for email automation platform and content creation.
KPIs:
  • Email open rate > 25%
  • Click-through > 5% on informational campaigns
  • 15%+ conversion of trial users to paid
  • Reduction in churn (reactivate X% of inactive accounts)
  • 20% of upgrades influenced by email touchpoints
